Zoom Example

Given: Multiple image sets with an initial main image and zero or more alternate images

On desktop: On selection of an image set, populate the main and alternate images into the layout. On mousewheel over the main image, zoom in or out. On click of an alternate image, reset the zoom level to 0 and change the source of the main image.

On mobile: display main and alternate images in a slider with swipe capability. On tap of image, expand it to fill the viewport and allow gestures for pinch and zoom and dragging.
